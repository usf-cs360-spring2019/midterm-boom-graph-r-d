Interpretation

We can see the different types of incident calls that happen, depending on the location of the incident. Some Zipcodes, for instance 94158 has a higher percentage of life-threatening calls, while 94108 highest percentage of fires.

Discussion

With this data, we can see what types of incidents are most likely to happen at a particular Zipcode, and understand why those areas might be more prone to fires or crime.
